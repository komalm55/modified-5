Leave Letter System

Apply for leave easily through a digital system that tracks requests and approvals.

Process Hierarchy:
  1. Student: Submits a leave request with details (name, roll number, reason, duration, etc.).
  2. Assigned Teacher: Reviews and approves/rejects the leave request.
  3. Head of Department (HOD): If approved by the teacher, the request moves to the HOD for further verification.
  4. Warden: The final approval is given by the hostel warden.
  5. Security Desk: Once approved, the security team gets notified with the student's unique leave ID.
Key Features:
  • Real-time tracking of leave status.
  • Automated notifications to stakeholders.
  • History log of all leave requests.
×

Laundry System

Submit laundry requests and track the status of your clothes with a simple form-based system.

Process Hierarchy:
  1. Student: Selects laundry services, enters details (items, quantity, preferences), and submits a request.
  2. Laundry Staff: Reviews the request, assigns a collection time, and updates the system.
  3. Pickup & Processing: Clothes are collected, cleaned, and tracked in the system.
  4. Notification: Student receives real-time updates on the status (In Progress, Ready for Pickup).
  5. Delivery/Pickup: The student collects the laundry, and the request is marked as completed.
Key Features:
  • Online laundry request submission.
  • Live tracking of laundry status.
  • Automated reminders for collection.
×

Report Forms

This form allows students to submit complaints, maintenance requests, or general reports directly to the warden.

Process Hierarchy:
  1. Student: Fills out the report form with necessary details (issue type, description, urgency).
  2. Warden: Reviews the submitted report and verifies the details.
  3. Warden Decision: Takes appropriate action (approves maintenance, forwards to concerned staff, or provides a resolution).
  4. Student: Receives an update on the report status.
Key Features:
  • Easy submission process for complaints and requests.
  • Direct communication between student and warden.
  • Track report status in real-time.
  • Quick resolution and feedback system.
×

AI Room Recommendations

Get AI-based suggestions for room selection based on preferences and availability.

Process Hierarchy:
  1. Student: Inputs preferences such as room type, location, and roommates.
  2. AI System: Analyzes preferences and available rooms to generate recommendations.
  3. Student: Reviews and selects a recommended room.
  4. System Confirmation: Confirms the room allocation and updates the database.
Key Features:
  • Personalized room recommendations.
  • Real-time availability check.
  • Automated room allocation process.
